New Version - Transition Information

ISB-CGC is doing some re-engineering, and will release a new version of the Web Application in the coming weeks.

Existing accounts and saved cohorts will not be migrated to the new version of the system, however the current version will remain available for some time to assist you in re-creating your data in the new version. We recommend you look at your currently saved cohorts in preparation for manually recreating them on the new system.

Workbooks and visualizations are slated for retirement and will not be on the new system. If you would like to see these features continued, please reach out to us at feedback@isb-cgc.org. We would love to hear about your feedback about our systems.
